About Honggao Cao

Quantitative analytics, model governance, and practical judgment across AI and everyday reasoning.

Honggao Cao's work draws on a background in quantitative analytics, model validation, risk governance, and applied reasoning. His experience in financial-sector analytics and model governance has focused on the practical problem of making decisions when models, evidence, assumptions, and institutional responsibilities all matter.

That background informs his current interest in artificial intelligence, governance, uncertainty, and judgment. Rather than treating AI as only a technical subject, his projects ask how people can understand AI systems, evaluate AI-enabled decisions, and keep human reasoning visible when tools become more powerful.

This site brings together three completed projects that follow naturally from that work. Practical AI Sense introduces AI concepts, labs, and governance-oriented learning material. Practical AI Evaluation develops an evidence-centered framework and interactive workbench for reviewing AI-enabled decisions. Practical Life Sense explores family- and story-based reasoning through stories, questions, and discussion prompts.

Together, the projects share a practical question: how can people reason more clearly when technology, evidence, uncertainty, and everyday judgment overlap?
