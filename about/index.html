About Honggao Cao

Three completed projects on AI literacy, AI evaluation, and family- and story-based reasoning.

This site brings together three completed projects. Practical AI Sense introduces AI concepts, labs, and governance-oriented learning material. Practical AI Evaluation / PAE Workbench develops an evidence-centered framework for reviewing AI-enabled decisions. Practical Life Sense explores family- and story-based reasoning through stories, questions, and discussion prompts.

The projects share a practical question: how can people reason more clearly when technology, evidence, uncertainty, and everyday judgment overlap? They approach that question from different directions: learning how AI works, evaluating when AI-assisted decisions can be trusted, and practicing judgment through family-readable stories and reflection.

Honggao's background in quantitative analytics, model validation, risk governance, and applied reasoning informs the work in these independent projects. They are educational and prototype materials, not professional advice, approval, certification, validation, or proof of safety.
